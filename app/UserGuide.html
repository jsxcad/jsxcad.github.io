Circle (disc)

Circles are approximated as surfaces delimeted by regular polygons.

Properly speaking what is produced here are discs. The circle perimeter can be extracted via outline().

circle()

circle(10)

circle({ radius: 10,
         resolution: 8 })

circle({ diameter: 20,
         resolution: 16 })

Cube (cuboid)

Generates cuboids.

cube()

cube(10)

cube({ radius: 8 })

cube({ diameter: 16 })

cube({ corner1: [0, 0, 0],
       corner2: [10, 10, 10] })

Cylinder

Generates cylinders.

cylinder()

cylinder(10, 2)

cylinder({ radius: 2,
           height: 10,
           resolution: 8 })

cylinder({ diameter: 6,
           height: 8,
           resolution: 16 })
